Complete Printer Repairing Process

Arya Info System provides professional printer repair services including door-step service and workshop repair. We ensure complete cleaning, parts inspection, and performance testing.

Step 1: Door-Step Inspection & Basic Repair

  • On-site printer inspection
  • Basic issue diagnosis
  • Minor part adjustment
  • Roller & cartridge checking
  • Basic cleaning and testing

Step 2: Office Workshop Repair (If Required)

  • Printer pickup from customer location
  • Complete internal opening of printer
  • Deep cleaning of internal components
  • Faulty parts identification
  • Replacement after customer approval

Step 3: Full Printer Cleaning Process

  • Outer body cleaning
  • Dust removal using air blower
  • Roller cleaning & lubrication
  • Laser unit cleaning (for laser printers)
  • Ink system flushing (for inkjet printers)

Step 4: Parts Cleaning & Maintenance

  • Drum unit cleaning
  • Fuser unit inspection
  • Cartridge area cleaning
  • Sensor cleaning
  • Gear mechanism checking

Step 5: Final Testing & Delivery

  • Test printing
  • Print quality check
  • Paper jam testing
  • Final performance verification
  • Safe delivery to customer
