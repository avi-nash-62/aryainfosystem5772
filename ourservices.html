01. Advanced Cartridge & Toner Solutions

We provide a sustainable and cost-effective alternative to expensive OEM cartridges without compromising on print quality. Our process involves ultrasonic cleaning, drum inspection, and high-density toner refilling. We also offer Brand New Compatible Toners and our signature Exchange Program: bring your empty shell and leave with a ready-to-print unit instantly.

02. Precision Printer Repairing

Our technicians are experts in Laser, Inkjet, and Dot Matrix technologies. We handle fuser replacements, scanner unit repairs, logic board troubleshooting, and routine preventative maintenance. We ensure your office "uptime" is maximized by providing standby printers during lengthy repairs.

03. Laptop & Computer Engineering

From high-end gaming rigs to essential office workstations, we provide chip-level motherboard repairs, RAM/SSD upgrades, hinge repairs, and specialized data recovery services. We support all major brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, and ASUS.

04. Corporate Rental Systems

Avoid heavy capital expenditure with our rental model. We provide high-configuration laptops, desktops, and heavy-duty multifunction printers for events, short-term projects, or long-term office setups, complete with on-site support.
