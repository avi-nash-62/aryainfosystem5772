Cartridge & Toner Excellence

Our cartridge refilling isn't just a "top-up"—it's a complete restoration process:

  • Full Disassembly: We strip the cartridge to its core components.
  • Deep Cleaning: Removal of waste toner and cleaning of the internal blades.
  • Premium Refill: We use high-grade chemical toner for OEM-quality prints.
  • Quality Testing: Every unit is tested for page yield and darkness before delivery.
